Quinoa Salad

Quinoa salad is a healthy and nutritious meal that is great for multiple occasions. It is a well mixed combination of veggies, carbs and protein to give your body fuel for the whole day.

So lets start off on the main part of the dish, the quinoa which is a super-food in it self. Then the chickpeas also know as garbanzo beans packed with protein. The cucumbers, cumin, tomato, ect are also going to give your body all the vitamin and nutrition you need.

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Diced red onion
  • Diced red tomato
  • Garbanzo Beans / Chickpeas
  • Bell Pepper
  • Cilantro
  • Diced cucumber
  • cumin
  • Lime juice
  • Olive Olive
  • Paprika
  • Salt&Pepper

    • Steps:

    1. Cook the Quinoa with two cups of water to one cup of quinoa
    2. Dice up your veggies into a large mixing bowl
    3. In a smaller separate bowl mix your dressing ingredients

      4. (Lime juice, Olive oil, cumin, Paprika, salt & pepper)

    4. Mix your dressing and veggies together
    5. Once your Quinoa is finished mix it into your larger mixing bowl with your other ingredients
    6. Last and final step: ENJOY!
