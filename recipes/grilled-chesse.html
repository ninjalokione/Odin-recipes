Grilled Cheese

The perfect lunch for everybody, is none other then the Classic grilled cheese. Wether you have it alone or with a hot bowl of tomato soup it is the perfect meal for anyone.

This meal is quick and easy snack for kids, adults, you name with with only a couple delicious ingredients.

  • Bread, preferably a flat bread, but use your imagination
  • Cheese, again this is up to you everyone's cheesiness is different
  • Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat up a pan to medium to high heat
  2. Cut or scoop butter into the pan depending on how much you like, but not enough to make bread soggy
  3. place two slices of bread into the pan
  4. once slightly toasted turn bread over and add your favorite cheese
  5. Place one slice of bread on the other. Cheese to cheese
  6. continue flipping until cheese is melted and bread is toasted

    7. If you are working with a cheese that doesn't melt well, add a little water to the pan and cover.

  7. As always last and final step: ENJOY!
